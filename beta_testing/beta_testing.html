Pre-release product review — thank you for taking part

What we're asking you to do

You've been invited to try Scales Academy before it's released to the public. You don't need any technical background. Just use the app as you normally would and tell us what you think.

Installing the app

You will receive an invitation email from Apple containing a View in TestFlight link.

To install:

  1. Install TestFlight from the App Store — you only need to do this once.
  2. Tap View in TestFlight in the invitation email. TestFlight will open and guide you through installing the app.
  3. Once installed, Scales Academy appears on your home screen and can be opened directly from there like any other app.
